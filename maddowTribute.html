Synopsis - Rachel Anne Maddow is a political commentator, a news host, and a book author. Maddow hosts the most popular show - The Rachel Maddow Show, in its prime hour slot on MSNBC. Maddow has many first: First of 'openly gay' female news anchor working among extremely competitive cables news. She is also the first gay female being awareded a Rhodes Scholarship. Maddow later earned a Doctor of Pilosophy in politics from Oxford University. Lastly, with her excellence at work, Maddow has been awarded 3 times of Emmy Awards: one in 2011 and two in 2017!
